About Us!

Our site is meant to help students meet new people within their classes. By creating groups, students can make connections within their classes and study together. For many, studying together is a good way to commit to improving one's capabilities and the content presented in classes. In other cases, studying together can create a support net within classes, allowing students to have designated people for help or support within classes and for commiting to reviewing information in classes.

Our site has many features, such as:

  • Being able to create your own profile see profiles of your classmates!
  • Being able to choose which classmates you want to match with!
  • Being able to form study groups with students in your classes
  • Being able to enter tags for your hobbies and interests to connect to students with similar interests!
  • ...and so much more!
